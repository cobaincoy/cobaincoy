## Is Pearson A Monthly Subscription? [Solved]
Pearson offers two plans for Pearson+. The single plan is $9.99 a month with a four-month minimum ($39.96 for four months). This subscription option gives subscribers access to one eText title on up to three authorized devices. The multi plan is $14.99 a month with a four-month minimum ($59.96 for four months).9 Aug 2021
