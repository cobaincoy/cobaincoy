## Should We Apply Clutch While Braking? [Solved]
While braking, you should always depress the clutch. Always depress the clutch when braking, a tip majorly for the new learners. This is one of the most common scenarios wherein people do apply the brakes but forget to disengage the clutch in-turn stalling the car.3 Oct 2020
